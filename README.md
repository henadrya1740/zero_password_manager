## 🔎 What is zero_password_manager?

zero_password_manager is an open-source app that helps you keep your passwords and crypto seed phrases safe. It stores your data only on your device. It does not send your information to the cloud or any server. You control your password vault entirely.

The app is built with Flutter, which means it works smoothly on multiple devices. You get a clean, simple interface that focuses on privacy and security.

## 🎯 Key Features

- Store passwords, PINs, and crypto seed phrases.
- Data saved locally, never uploaded online.
- Offline-first design for more privacy.
- Easy to use with a clear, simple layout.
- Works on Windows and other platforms.
- Open source – anyone can check the code for security.

## 👨‍💻 How to Use zero_password_manager

### Add a password

- Click the **Add** button.
- Enter the website or service name.
- Type your username or email.
- Enter your password.
- Save the entry.

### Add a crypto seed phrase

- Click **Add**.
- Choose **Seed Phrase** from the entry type.
- Enter the phrase carefully.
- Confirm and save.

### Organize your data

You can categorize your entries, label them, or search for any password quickly inside the app.

### Backup your data

Since zero_password_manager works offline, backing up your data is your responsibility.

- Use the backup option in the app to save your encrypted vault file.
- Store the backup on a USB drive or external hard drive.
- Keep the backup file in a secure place.

## 🔒 Security Notes

zero_password_manager does not store your data in the cloud. This reduces exposure to hacking risks related to online storage.

All your data is encrypted with strong algorithms before saving it to your device.

The app never transmits your information over the internet without your permission.

If your vault file is damaged, restore from your latest backup copy.
