Your Data. Your Server. Your Rules.

A premium, self-hosted password manager with Zero-Knowledge encryption and stunning Cyberpunk aesthetics.

☁️

Pure Self-Hosting

Your passwords never touch the cloud. Host your own data on your own hardware using our lightweight FastAPI backend.

🛡️

Hardened 2FA

Mandatory TOTP setup during registration. Critical actions are gated by one-time passwords with replay protection.

🎨

Dynamic Themes

Switch between Midnight Dark, Cyberpunk, and Glassmorphism themes. A UI designed to be as beautiful as it is secure.

🔓

Fully Open & Custom

The code is yours. Use our FastAPI/SQLite stack or plug in your own backend and database. Total freedom to audit and modify.

📱

iOS, Android & Web

Built with Flutter for a truly native experience. Deploy on mobile or access via any browser with 100% feature parity.

🧠

Zero-Knowledge

Encryption happens on your device. Your master password never leaves the app, ensuring even the server host can't see your data.

Hardened Infrastructure

🔐 Encryption: AES-256-GCM
🔑 KDF: Argon2id
📱 App: Flutter / Dart
🐍 Backend: FastAPI / Python
💾 Database: SQLAlchemy / SQLite
🛡️ Auth: JWT + TOTP
